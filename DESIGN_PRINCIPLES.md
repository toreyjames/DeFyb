# DeFyb Design Principles

## 1. Truth-first billing

Never fabricate clinical documentation.
Only present suggestions grounded in encounter evidence.

## 2. Doctor remains in control

AI and rules suggest.
Clinicians approve.

## 3. Evidence-based coding

Every code recommendation must include explicit, auditable rationale.

## 4. Workflow speed

Provider interaction should remain under 10 seconds per routine encounter review.

## 5. Revenue transparency

Always show estimated revenue impact and correction value.
